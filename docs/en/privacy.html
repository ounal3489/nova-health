Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Nova does NOT collect any personal data. All your health data is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to any server.

1. Data Collection

Nova does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The app operates entirely offline. There are:

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No crash reporting services
Türkçe

2. Local Data Storage

All data you enter into Nova, including period logs, symptoms, moods, and notes, is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it (for example, generating a PDF report).

3. Apple HealthKit Integration

Nova offers optional integration with Apple HealthKit. If you choose to enable this integration:

  • Data read from or written to HealthKit is governed by Apple's privacy policies
  • Nova only accesses HealthKit data categories that you explicitly authorize
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through your device's Settings
Türkçe

4. In-App Purchases

Nova offers in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Nova does not collect or have access to your payment information. All purchase transactions are handled by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policy.

5. Biometric Security

Nova offers an optional biometric lock feature (Face ID / Touch ID) to protect access to the app. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's secure enclave and is never accessible to Nova.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from within the app's Settings. When you delete your data, it is permanently removed from your device. Since no data is stored on external servers, deletion is immediate and complete.

Uninstalling Nova from your device will also remove all locally stored data.

7. Third-Party Data Sharing

Nova does not share any data with third parties. There are no advertising partners, analytics providers, or data brokers involved in the operation of this app.

8. Children's Privacy

Nova does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, no special provisions are required under COPPA or similar regulations.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Since Nova does not collect any contact information, we are unable to notify you directly of changes.
